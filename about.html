About Us

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) is Malaysia largest university, offering over 500 programmes across 34 campuses and 14 faculties nationwide. Equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, UiTM provides accessible education from Foundation to PhD levels, fostering talent and shaping future leaders.

Recognized globally, UiTM ranks among the top universities in the QS and Times Higher Education rankings. It has received numerous accolades, including the Reader’s Digest Trusted Brand Gold Award and the BrandLaureate BestBrands Awards. With over 770,000 alumni excelling in various fields, UiTM is committed to driving innovation, sustainability, and nation-building, aiming to be a globally renowned institution by 2025.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution that inspires and empowers individuals to achieve excellence in education and innovation, fostering a sustainable future for society.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality education and research opportunities that nurture creativity, leadership, and lifelong learning in a diverse and inclusive environment.
